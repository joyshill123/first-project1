Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about DevConf 2026.

Who should attend DevConf 2026?

DevConf is open to software developers, students, engineers, founders, designers, and anyone interested in technology.

What's included with my ticket?

Your ticket includes access to keynote sessions, workshops, networking events, refreshments, and conference materials.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. All attendees will receive access to session recordings after the conference.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Yes. Tickets can be transferred to another attendee before the event begins.

Is food provided?

Lunch, coffee breaks, and light refreshments are included throughout the conference.

How can I contact the organizers?

You can reach our support team anytime through the Contact page or by email.
